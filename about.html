Nicolle Cahill

Design is the art of storytelling within a home. Based in Boise, Idaho, Nicolle founded FINA Home Design to help sellers and homeowners realize the full potential of their spaces.

With an eye for modern mountain aesthetics and organic textures, Nicolle creates environments that don't just look beautiful—they feel like home.

Whether preparing a vacant listing for the Treasure Valley market or refining a private residence in Eagle, her goal is to highlight the soul of the property and inspire an immediate connection with every visitor.
